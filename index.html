Yamka is a messaging service that focuses on users' privacy and feedback

Private

We don't collect your data beyond such that's needed to provide the service. And we don't give it away - for free or not.

Free

All official clients, SDKs and libraries are open source and peer-reviewed, which enables superior security, stability and feature set

Secure

Your direct messages are secured using End-to-End Encryption (E2EE), meaning nobody (even us!) except you and your penfriend can read them.

Modern and featureful

Find a group of people by your interests or get invited into one. Chat in multiple channels. Perform administrative commands and choose other people who have that ability. Diversify your experience using automated bots with deep integration. And so much more!

Lightning fast

Starts up in less than 3 seconds on an 8 year old laptop and works faster thanks to custom software solutions

Supports voice chat

Person-to-person voice communications are secured using E2EE too!

Nonprofit

We run off of generous donations of people that make us possible. Not being one of them is totally okay, but support is much appreciated. It doesn't have to be money. Computing power, moral support and spreading the word are ways to help us too!
